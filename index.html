InyassTech Brief Description

Inyass Technology Solutions Limited is a trusted technology solutions provider specializing in computer networking, website development, corporate branding, IT consulting, import & export of computer units and accessories, as well as professional manpower training. Inyass Technology Solutions Ltd formally Inyass PC Solutions Ventures is an Information Technology (IT) solution company establish and dully registered with Corporate Affairs Commission as a Limited Liability Company. The Company is successfully operating in Niger State and Nigeria at large on both small- and large-scale services in Information and Communication Technology, Web Designs (Web browsers, ftp, e-mail, associated hardware, Internet service providers, and so forth), computer training, supply and installation of computers alongside modern accessories, electronic gadgets, stationary supplies and IT collaboration, printing and publishing services. With the present market boom and concurrent development in IT across world, and the desire for upgrade in computer and information technology, the Company has successfully expanded its target from individual small scale clients to larger and bigger industrial, commercial and institutional clients. Thus, business plan, vision and goals have tasked us to making this transition successful and ensure exceptional quality service delivery to our esteemed clients. Every sector is today IT compliant, but the high and modern technology sector is the eye of the storm. The chances of wider opportunity in the sector stands a better chance in the modern world. This is simply because it defines and refines the future, while respect and discipline are crucial, notwithstanding, the inherent problems of quacks and substandard products becomes a great challenge in the industry. Inyass Technology Solutions Ltd is committed to providing customers with modern, inventive and high quality products and services that stand the test of time and are in fact, second to none. We have the "leading edge" when it comes to the technical knowhow with respect to remote computer repairs, networking, software development and installations, etc.
